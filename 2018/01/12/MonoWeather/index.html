MonoWeather

MonoWeather is an elegant monochromatic (iOS) Weather App that automatically fetch user’s location and find the local weather report. The design enhances the most important information Temperature with weather information, wind speed, humidity and temperature range for today. By clicking temperature, more detailed temperatures of the future will be shown.




MonoWeather 是一个优雅的单色调 (iOS) 天气 App 。 它可以自动的获取用户的地理位置天气信息。它的设计侧重于凸显最重要的信息，也就是气温。当然还有天气状况、风速、湿度以及当天气温的变化区间。点击气温数字，它还可以显示更多未来几天的气温和天气状况。
